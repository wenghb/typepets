About TypePets

TypePets is a free, open-source typing practice application designed for children. It runs entirely in your web browser.

Use

TypePets is provided free of charge for personal, educational use. You may use it to practice typing at home or in educational settings.

TypePets is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. We do our best to make it work well, but cannot guarantee it will be error-free.

Data

All data is stored locally on your device. If you clear your browser data, your progress will be lost. We recommend backing up important progress.
