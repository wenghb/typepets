Frequently Asked Questions

Is TypePets really free?

Yes, 100% free. No signup, no premium tier, no ads, no in-app purchases. Every feature is available right now.

Does TypePets collect my child's data?

No. All progress data is stored in the browser's localStorage and never sent anywhere. There are no accounts, no servers, and no tracking. We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-first tool that doesn't use cookies or track individuals. Your child's data stays on their device.

What age is TypePets for?

TypePets is designed for kids ages 7–12 who are learning to touch type. The difficulty auto-adjusts, so younger beginners and more advanced typists both get an appropriate challenge.

What keyboard layout does it support?

TypePets currently supports QWERTY — the standard layout on most English-language keyboards.

Can I use it on iPad or tablet?

Yes, TypePets is fully responsive and works on iPads and tablets. However, a physical keyboard is recommended for the best typing practice experience.

How does the pet system work?

Every typing session earns XP. Your pet starts as an egg and evolves through 6 stages: Egg → Baby → Kid → Teen → Adult → Master. You can also feed it and play with it to keep it happy.

Is there a parent dashboard?

The built-in Dashboard shows typing speed over time, accuracy heatmaps by key, and achievement progress. Parents and kids can review it together to track improvement.
