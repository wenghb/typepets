Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

No Data Collection

TypePets is a children's typing practice application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to any server.

Local Storage Only

All your progress, settings, and data are stored locally on your device using your web browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

No Cookies

We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.

Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how our site is used. This is a privacy-first analytics tool that does not use cookies, does not track individuals, and does not collect personal information.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts to display text. Google's privacy policy applies to the loading of these font files. No other third-party services are used.

Children's Privacy

TypePets is designed for children. We are committed to protecting children's privacy. Since we collect no data whatsoever, there is no personal information at risk.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the project's repository.
